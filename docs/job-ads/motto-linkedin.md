## Description

**The way you speak English is exactly what AI needs to hear.**

We're looking for native English speakers across 7 countries to record natural, unscripted audio using their iPhone. Your everyday speech — your accent, your rhythm, your way of telling a story — helps AI models understand how real people actually talk.

**What you'll do:**
Use your iPhone to record yourself speaking naturally for at least 2 minutes per session. You can record solo or with up to 3 other people. No scripts — just speak the way you normally would. Talk about a news event, riff on a topic you love like you're recording a podcast, or tell stories using fictitious names and places.

**Why it matters:**
English sounds different in Sydney, Dublin, Delhi, and Auckland. AI needs to understand all of it. Your recordings help build speech models that work for English speakers everywhere.

**Pay (per completed session):**
- $21.60 — Australia, Ireland
- $21.31 — United Kingdom
- $18.38 — New Zealand
- $12.00 — South Africa
- $9.60 — Singapore
- $3.00 — India

**Requirements:**
- Native English speaker
- iPhone owner
- Located in one of the 7 eligible countries
- Comfortable speaking naturally on everyday topics

No experience needed. No app download. Your voice is the qualification.
