## Job Description

### Your native language helps AI understand how real people speak

OneForma is looking for native speakers to join paid, remote audio discussion sessions. Your natural, unscripted conversation helps train AI speech and language models to understand real human speech — across languages, accents, and speaking styles.

This is a short-term project with flexible scheduling. No prior experience required.

---

### What the role involves

You'll join a group of 3 native speakers for a recorded, 30-minute online discussion on everyday topics — similar to a podcast or talk show. The conversation is natural and unscripted. One speaker acts as the host/moderator while the others participate as guests.

**Specifically, you will:**
- Join 30-minute recorded audio sessions online with 2 other native speakers
- Discuss provided topics in a natural, conversational way — no script
- Take on either a moderator (host) or guest speaker role
- Participate in 3 to 5 sessions total
- Submit a brief summary after each session

---

### Pay

Hourly rates vary by language:

**$40.00/hr:**
Danish, Dutch, English (Ireland), Finnish, French (Canada), Norwegian

**$35.00/hr:**
French (Belgium), German (Austria), German (Germany), German (Switzerland)

**$32.00/hr:**
English (United Kingdom), French (France), Spanish (Spain)

Payment is based on approved recordings. Sessions are 30 minutes each. You'll complete 3–5 sessions total.

---

### Requirements

**Must have:**
- Native-level fluency in one of the listed languages
- Comfort speaking naturally and spontaneously on everyday topics
- Availability for scheduled 30-minute sessions (3–5 sessions total)
- A quiet recording environment
- A device with a microphone (headset preferred)
- Reliable internet connection

**No experience required.** Your native fluency is the qualification.

---

### Why join

- **Paid hourly** — $32–$40/hr depending on your language
- **Fully remote** — join from anywhere with an internet connection
- **Flexible scheduling** — sessions are scheduled around your availability
- **Meaningful contribution** — your authentic speech helps build AI that works for real people
- **Short commitment** — 3 to 5 sessions, 30 minutes each
