## Description

**Your native language is exactly what AI is missing.**

We're looking for native speakers of 13 languages to join paid, podcast-style audio discussions that help train AI speech and language models.

**What you'll do:**
You'll join a small group of 3 native speakers for a natural, unscripted 30-minute discussion on everyday topics — similar to a podcast episode. One speaker hosts the conversation while the others contribute as guests. Sessions are fully remote and recorded.

You'll participate in 3–5 sessions total, scheduled at times that work for you.

**Why it matters:**
AI systems learn from real conversations — not scripts. Your natural turn-taking, expressions, and way of speaking in your native language help speech models understand how people actually talk. This project supports more accurate, inclusive AI across languages and dialects.

**Compensation (per hour):**
- $40/hr — Danish, Dutch, English (Ireland), Finnish, French (Canada), Norwegian
- $35/hr — French (Belgium), German (Austria / Germany / Switzerland)
- $32/hr — English (UK), French (France), Spanish (Spain)

Payment is based on approved recordings.

**Requirements:**
- Native speaker of one of the listed languages
- Comfortable speaking naturally and spontaneously on everyday topics
- Available for scheduled 30-minute sessions (3–5 total)
- Quiet recording environment with a device and microphone (headset preferred)
- Reliable internet connection

No prior experience needed. Your native fluency is the expertise.

**Apply through the link for your specific language at oneforma.com/jobs/husky**
