## Job Description

### The way you speak English is exactly what AI needs to hear

OneForma is looking for native English speakers across 7 countries to record natural, unscripted audio on their iPhone. Your everyday speech helps train AI systems to understand how real people communicate — across accents, dialects, and speaking styles.

No scripts. No studio. No experience needed. Just you, your iPhone, and a few minutes.

---

### What you'll do

Use your iPhone to record yourself speaking naturally in English for at least 2 minutes per session. You can record alone or with up to 3 other people.

**Pick from topics like:**
- Talk about a news event you've been following
- Riff on a topic you love, as if recording a podcast
- Tell stories using fictitious names and places

The goal is authentic, everyday speech — the kind of conversation you'd have at a coffee shop or on a voice note to a friend.

### Requirements

**Must have:**
- Native English speaker
- iPhone
- Located in one of the 7 eligible countries (Australia, New Zealand, India, UK, Ireland, South Africa, Singapore)

**Not required:**

- No app download
- No special equipment beyond your iPhone

### Why this matters

English sounds different everywhere it's spoken. AI needs to understand the real thing — not just scripted studio recordings. Your voice, your accent, and the way you naturally express yourself help build speech models that work for English speakers around the world.
