## Long version (full copy — for platforms with no limits)

### Your native language is exactly what AI is missing

AI speech models learn from real conversations — not actors reading scripts. They need authentic group discussions with natural interruptions, reactions, and the rhythm of how people actually talk in their native language.

That is where you come in.

You will join a small group of 3 native speakers for a recorded, 30-minute online discussion on everyday topics provided in advance. The format is similar to a podcast episode — one speaker acts as the host/moderator while the others participate as guests. The conversation should feel casual, engaging, and authentic.

Sessions are fully remote and scheduled at times that work for you. You will participate in 3 to 5 sessions total.

---

### What you will do

- Join a 30-minute recorded audio session online with 2 other native speakers
- Discuss provided topics in a natural, unscripted way
- Take on a moderator (host) or guest speaker role
- Keep the conversation natural — like a real podcast or talk show
- Submit a brief episode summary after each session

---

### Compensation

Hourly pay varies by language:

| Tier 2 | French (Belgium), German (Austria), German (Germany), German (Switzerland) | $35/hr |
| Tier 3 | English (UK), French (France), Spanish (Spain) | $32/hr |

Payment is based on approved recordings.

---

### How it works

1. Apply — select your language and confirm eligibility
2. Get matched — you will be grouped with 2 other native speakers
3. Review topics — receive everyday discussion topics in advance
4. Join the session — connect online for a 30-minute recorded discussion
5. Submit your summary — provide a brief recap after the session
6. Get paid — receive payment for approved recordings

---

### Requirements

- Native speaker of one of the listed languages
- Comfortable speaking naturally and spontaneously on everyday topics
- Available for scheduled 30-minute sessions (3–5 total)
- Quiet recording environment
- Device with a microphone (headset preferred)
- Reliable internet connection

No prior experience or resume required. Your native fluency is the expertise.

---

### Why this matters

Your authentic conversation helps AI systems understand how real people speak — across languages, dialects, and everyday speaking styles. This project contributes to more accurate, inclusive speech recognition and language models that work for everyone.
